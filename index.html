✨ Features Guide

🔍

Analyst Mode

Scans the card and draws a cyan bounding box on every detected symbol, plus a live frequency list below. Updates in real time while the camera is running.

🎯

Classic Mode

Only highlights duplicate symbols (those appearing 2+ times) with color-coded neon glow boxes. Each duplicate class gets its own distinct color. Best for quickly spotting the match.

⚡

Speed Mode

Race the clock! Tap every duplicate symbol on the keyboard as fast as possible. Timer runs until all duplicates are found. Tracks accuracy, streak, and logs your top 5 session times.

🃏🃏

Two-Card Mode

Upload two cards — the AI finds which symbols appear on both. Shared symbols are highlighted in matching colors on each card. This is actual Spot It! rules!

📷

Live Camera

Opens your rear camera for real-time detection. Start/Stop and Freeze & Analyze buttons appear. Detection style follows the active game mode.

📸

Freeze & Analyze

Captures the current frame and runs full analysis. In Speed Mode, this starts the timed round. Supports optional 3-2-1 countdown first.

🖼️

Photo Upload

Upload any image from your gallery. Works in all 4 modes. History thumbnails show last 8 cards with green (match found) or red (no match) badges.

🏷️

Card Difficulty Badge

After every scan a difficulty badge appears based on duplicate symbol count: 😴 Easy (1 dup) → 🙂 Chill → 😤 Tricky → 😱 Intense → 🤯 Chaos (7+ dups).

⏱

Live Timer

Starts the moment the card is analyzed. Stops when you tap the last correct symbol. Shows elapsed time in the HUD in real time.

🎯

Accuracy Tracking

Wrong taps vs total taps shown as a percentage in the HUD. Updates live every tap.

🔥

Streak Counter

Increments when you complete a round with zero wrong taps. Resets on any wrong tap. Toast notifications fire at streak milestones.

🏅

Session Top Times

Your 5 fastest correct rounds this session, shown above the keyboard with medal icons and accuracy per entry.

💡

Hint

Reveals the current target symbol with an orange glow box on the card and highlights the correct key on the keyboard.

📊

Symbol Heatmap

Tap the 📊 button to open a full heatmap of all 57 symbols. Cells are colored from gray (never seen) to vivid green (seen many times). Shows total detections, unique count, never-seen count, and most-seen symbol.

🎨

Themes

Tap 🎨 to cycle through 5 themes: Default Sky → 🌊 Ocean → 🌅 Sunset → 🌲 Forest → 🌌 Space. Space theme adds 120 animated twinkling stars.

🌙

Dark Mode

Switches to a dark night theme. Works independently of the color theme.

✨

Glow Boxes

Neon glow around detection boxes. In Classic mode each duplicate class gets its own color. In Analyst mode all boxes are cyan.

🔊

Sound

Scan beep, victory chime, wrong-tap buzz, and countdown ticks.
