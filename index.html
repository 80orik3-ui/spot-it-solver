✨ Features Guide

📷

Start / Stop Camera

Opens your rear camera with real-time symbol detection on every frame. Green boxes = duplicates, red = unique symbols.

📸

Freeze & Analyze

Captures the current frame, stops camera, runs full analysis — counts symbols, shows duplicates, fires confetti on match, saves to history.

⏳

Countdown Freeze

When ON, pressing Freeze shows a 3-2-1 countdown before capturing. Gives you time to hold the card perfectly still.

📊

Live Stats Bar

Shows in real time how many symbols and duplicates the AI sees, plus a frame counter.

🖼️

Upload Card

Upload a photo from your gallery. The AI instantly detects all symbols, draws boxes, and highlights duplicates.

📜

Card History

Thumbnails of your last 8 scanned cards. Green badge = duplicates found. Red badge = no match.

🔄

Reset

Clears the image, resets session stats, empties history.

🎯

Confidence Slider

How certain the AI must be before drawing a box. Lower = more (noisier). Higher = fewer but more accurate. Default 30%.

✨

Glow Boxes

Neon glow around detection boxes — green for duplicates, red for unique.

🏷️

Labels

Shows symbol names on each box. Turn off to show boxes only.

🔢

Confidence %

Shows or hides the confidence score in labels (e.g. "dolphin 87%").

🌙

Dark Mode

Switches to a dark night theme.

🔊

Sound

Scan beep on detection, victory chime on match. Toggle to mute.
