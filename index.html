✨ Features Guide

📷

Start Camera

Opens your device camera (rear camera on phones). Point it at any Spot It! card and it detects symbols in real time with live bounding boxes.

📸

Freeze & Analyze

Only visible while the camera is running. Pauses the live feed and performs a full analysis — counts symbols, finds duplicates, updates scores, saves to history, and fires confetti if a match is found!

🖼️

Upload Card

Upload a photo of a Spot It! card from your device gallery. Instantly runs detection and shows you all symbols and any duplicates found.

🔄

Reset

Clears the current card, resets all session stats (scans, matches, total symbols) back to zero, and empties the card history panel.

🃏

Cards Scanned

Counts how many cards you've analyzed this session (uploads + camera freezes).

🎯

Matches Found

How many scanned cards had at least one duplicate symbol detected.

✨

Total Symbols

The cumulative count of all symbols detected across every card scanned this session.

🎯

Confidence Slider

Controls how certain the AI must be before drawing a box. Lower = more detections but more false positives. Higher = fewer but more accurate. Default is 30%.

✨

Glow Boxes

Adds a coloured neon glow around bounding boxes — green glow for duplicate symbols, red for unique ones. Turn off for a cleaner look.

🏷️

Labels

Shows the symbol name on each bounding box. Toggle off to see just the boxes without any text overlay.

🔢

Confidence %

Shows or hides the confidence percentage number inside each label (e.g. "dolphin 87%"). Turn off to show only the symbol name.

📜

History

Saves thumbnails of your last 8 scanned cards below the image area. Each thumbnail shows a green badge with the number of duplicates found (or red if none).

🌙

Dark Mode

Switches between light and dark themes for comfortable use in any lighting condition.

🔊

Sound

Plays a scan beep when analyzing, and a victory chime when duplicates are found. Toggle to mute all sounds.

📤

Share

Share this app's link with friends using your device's share menu, or copy it to clipboard.

📊

Stats

Shows a quick summary toast of your current session stats — scans, matches, and total symbols detected.
