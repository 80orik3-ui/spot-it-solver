✨ Features Guide

📷

Start / Stop Camera

Opens your device's rear camera and runs real-time symbol detection on every frame. Bounding boxes update live — green for duplicate symbols, red for unique ones.

📸

Freeze & Analyze

Tap to trigger a countdown (if enabled), then captures the exact frame, stops the camera, and runs a full analysis — counting all symbols, highlighting duplicates, updating your session stats, saving to history, and firing confetti on a match!

⏳

Countdown Freeze

When ON, pressing Freeze starts a 3-2-1 countdown on screen before capturing. Gives you time to hold the card perfectly steady before the shot is taken.

🃏

Min Symbols Gate

Set a minimum number of symbols that must be visible before a Freeze can run. Prevents analyzing a partial card view — only triggers when the whole card is in frame.

📊

Live Stats Bar

Shows in real time how many symbols and duplicates the AI currently sees on screen, plus a frame counter. Helps you know when the card is fully visible.

🖼️

Upload Card

Upload any photo from your gallery. The AI instantly detects all symbols, draws boxes, highlights duplicates, and shows results below.

📜

Card History

Thumbnails of your last 8 scanned cards. Green badge = number of duplicates found. Red badge = no match. Toggle history off to keep things clean.

🔄

Reset

Clears the current image, resets all session stats to zero, and empties the history panel.

🎯

Confidence Slider

How certain the AI must be before drawing a box. Lower = more detections (noisier). Higher = fewer but more accurate detections. Default is 30%.

✨

Glow Boxes

Adds a coloured neon glow around detection boxes. Green glow for duplicates, red for unique symbols. Turn off for a cleaner look.

🏷️

Labels

Shows the symbol name on each bounding box. Toggle off to show boxes only.

🔢

Confidence %

Shows or hides the confidence score inside labels (e.g. "dolphin 87%"). Turn off for just the symbol name.

🌙

Dark Mode

Switches the entire app to a dark theme for comfortable use in low-light conditions.

🔊

Sound

A beep plays on scan and a chime plays when a match is found. Toggle off to silence all sounds.
