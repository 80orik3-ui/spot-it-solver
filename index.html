✨ Features Guide

📷

Start / Stop Camera

Opens your device camera with real-time symbol detection. Bounding boxes update live on every frame — green for duplicates, red for unique symbols.

📸

Freeze & Analyze

Pauses the live feed and runs a full analysis on that exact frame — counts symbols, shows duplicates, fires confetti, updates session stats, and saves to history.

🤖

Auto-Freeze on Match

When active, the camera automatically freezes and analyzes the frame the moment the AI detects a duplicate symbol — no need to tap anything!

📊

Live Stats Bar

Shows real-time stats while the camera runs: how many symbols the AI currently sees, how many are duplicates, and a live recording indicator.

⏱️

Freeze Interval

Automatically freezes and analyzes a frame every N seconds while the camera is running. Great for hands-free scanning!

🎯

Min Duplicates Threshold

Only fire confetti and count a match if at least this many duplicate symbols are found. Reduces false positives from partial card views.

🖼️

Upload Card

Upload any photo of a Spot It! card from your gallery. Instantly detects all symbols and highlights duplicates.

📜

Card History

Thumbnails of your last 8 scanned cards (uploads + frozen camera frames). Green badge = number of duplicates found, red badge = no match.

🔄

Reset

Clears the current image, resets all session stats, and empties the history panel.

🎯

Confidence Slider

How certain the AI must be before drawing a box. Lower = more detections, possibly noisier. Higher = fewer but more accurate. Default 30%.

✨

Glow Boxes

Adds a neon glow around bounding boxes. Green glow for duplicate symbols, red for unique ones.

🏷️

Labels

Shows the symbol name on each bounding box. Turn off to see only the coloured boxes.

🔢

Confidence %

Shows or hides the confidence number inside labels (e.g. "dolphin 87%"). Turn off for just the name.

🌙

Dark Mode

Switches to a dark theme for comfortable use in low light.

🔊

Sound

Scan beep on detection, victory chime on match. Toggle to mute.
